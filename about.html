About EduManage Pro

Built for modern schools and coaching institutes to simplify management, improve communication, and empower learning.

Our Mission

EduManage Pro is a comprehensive school and coaching institute management platform designed to make the administrative process effortless. Our goal is to bridge the gap between students, teachers, and administrators using a single, unified platform.

We support GSEB & CBSE boards, both English and Gujarati mediums, and classes from 10th standard through competitive exam preparation (JEE & NEET).
